A League of CouchPotatoes

Here at CouchPotato our mission is to provide the best online movie streaming website with the most minimal amount of ads possible.

We make absolute no profit off of this website aside from what you choose to donate (Which we very much appreciate). This website was originally created to test our abilities as programmers and web developers.

We designed a bot to collect the links of public servers hosting the online videos you are watching. We do not host a single video on our servers. We are basically like a Google for online videos. With those links we collected we strip them down of as many pop up ads as possible. Yes, there may still be some ads, but trust us. We are saving you from more than you think!

It started off as a challenge of our abilities and slowly became spread to our friends and family. Now we are more than happy to share our entire project globally to whoever wants to watch movies without those annoying pop up ads that other sites have. (I won't name drop but you know)

Our goal will be to always remain free and open to the public. So we hope you enjoy our service and share this website with your friends too! :D
